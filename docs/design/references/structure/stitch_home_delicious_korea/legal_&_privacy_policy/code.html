Introduction

Welcome to Delicious Korea. These Terms of Service and Privacy Policy govern your use of our website and services related to our food tours in Seoul, South Korea.

By booking a tour or using our website, you agree to be bound by these terms. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring your culinary journey through Korea is safe and enjoyable.

1. Terms of Service

Service Provision: Delicious Korea provides guided food tours, cultural experiences, and dietary consulting for travelers visiting Seoul. Our guides are experts in Korean cuisine and local history.

Booking and Payment: Full payment is required at the time of booking to secure your spot. We accept major international credit cards and digital payment platforms.

Health & Safety: Participants must inform us of any severe food allergies or medical conditions prior to the tour. While we take every precaution, participants consume food at their own risk.

2. Privacy Policy

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number to facilitate your booking. Your data is stored securely and never sold to third parties.

  • Data Usage: We use your contact details to send tour reminders, meeting point locations, and a post-tour guide.
  • Cookies: Our website uses functional cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze traffic patterns.
  • Third Parties: We may share necessary details with local restaurant partners solely for reservation purposes.

3. Refund Policy

We understand that travel plans can change. Our refund structure is as follows:

Cancellation Time Refund Amount
More than 7 days notice 100% Refund
3 to 7 days notice 50% Refund
Less than 72 hours notice No Refund

Note: In case of tour cancellation by Delicious Korea due to extreme weather or unforeseen circumstances, a full refund will be issued.

4. User Conduct

To ensure a pleasant experience for everyone, we expect all tour participants to:

  • Respect local customs and Korean dining etiquette as explained by the guide.
  • Be punctual at the designated meeting point. Tours depart on time.
  • Refrain from inappropriate behavior toward guides, restaurant staff, or other guests.

Have questions about our policies?

Our legal team is here to help. Reach out to us for any clarification regarding your rights and our services.
