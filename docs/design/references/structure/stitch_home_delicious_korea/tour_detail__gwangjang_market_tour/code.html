Gwangjang Market Food Tour

Experience Authentic Seoul

Journey through one of Korea's oldest and most iconic traditional markets. Led by local culinary experts, you'll taste the legendary "Mayak Kimbap", crispy "Bindaetteok", and handmade "Kalguksu" while learning about the rich history of Seoul's food culture.
