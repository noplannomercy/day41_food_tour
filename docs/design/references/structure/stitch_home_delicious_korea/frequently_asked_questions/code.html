How can we help?

Find answers to our most common questions about food tours in Seoul.

Booking & Payment

How do I book a tour?

expand_more
You can book directly through our website by selecting your preferred tour and date. Once payment is confirmed, you'll receive a confirmation email with all details.

What payment methods are accepted?

expand_more
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, AMEX), PayPal, and Google Pay. For local residents, bank transfers can also be arranged upon request.

Can I book for a large group (10+ people)?

expand_more
Absolutely! For groups larger than 10, please contact us directly via our "Contact" page for special group rates and customized itineraries.

Dietary Restrictions

I have a nut allergy. Is it safe?

expand_more
Yes, but please let us know in advance. While Korean food uses sesame oil frequently, peanut or tree nut usage is less common but exists. Your guide will ensure all dishes served are safe for you.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

expand_more
We have a dedicated "Green Seoul" tour specifically for vegetarians. For our standard tours, options are limited due to the prevalence of fish sauce/shrimp paste, so we recommend the vegetarian-specific tour.

Meeting Points

Where do the tours start?

expand_more
Most tours meet at major subway stations like Jongno 3-ga, Myeongdong, or Hongdae. Exact locations with a map link will be sent in your confirmation email.

How do I recognize my guide?

expand_more
Our guides will be wearing a bright red "Delicious Korea" vest or holding a small company flag.

Cancellation Policy

What is the refund policy?

expand_more
- 100% refund for cancellations made 72 hours before the tour.
- 50% refund for cancellations made between 24 and 72 hours before.
- No refund for cancellations within 24 hours of the tour start time.
